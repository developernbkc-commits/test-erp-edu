Admissions controls

Grade-specific formsDifferent questions and documents by intake level.
Document checklistsBirth certificate, transfer certificate, health records, and proofs.
Counselling slotsOffer and reschedule interview or campus interaction slots.

Finance policy

Keep billing, waivers, and receipt experiences predictable and parent-friendly.

Installment plansOffer staged payments for eligible fee categories.
Concessions and waiversTrack discounts, scholarships, and approval routing.
Transport fee policyBundle, split, or reassign transport charges as needed.
Late fee automationTrigger reminders and auto-calculate penalties when policy applies.

Communications

Make outreach consistent, approved, and easy to audit.

Broadcast approvalsRoute principal-level approvals for sensitive messages.
Message templatesSave common notices for exams, fees, transport, and events.
Emergency alertsEscalate across app alerts, SMS, and email when needed.
Multi-language copyOffer translated communication for parent communities.

Operations & fault desk

Maintenance issue categoriesElectrical, HVAC, furniture, transport, safety, and IT.
SLA by severityDifferent response targets for critical vs routine issues.
Closure proof uploadCapture photos, receipts, and remarks before resolving tickets.
Vendor assignment workflowAssign vendors, track updates, and rate work quality.
