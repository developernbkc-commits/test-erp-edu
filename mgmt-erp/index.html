The demonstration focuses on the real friction schools face: scattered admissions follow-up, weak parent communication, delayed fee visibility, faculty workload confusion, and unresolved campus issues. Each page demonstrates a clear, elegant answer to those problems.

Real problems, mapped to visible solutions

This demo is not just a dashboard skin. It illustrates how a niche school ERP could solve the day-to-day operational pain points administrators actually care about.
